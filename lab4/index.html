Steps to start using your microwave

  • 1. Read the user’s manual to know more about your microwave
  • 2. Through away user’s manual because you know you will never use it again

  • 4. Put the food in a microwave-safe utensil only
  • 5. Ensure that the utensil is microwave-safe otherwise it will explode your microwave
  • 6. Put food inside the microwave
  • 7. Close the microwave
